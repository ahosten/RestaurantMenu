Our History

Crown Candy Kitchen was opened in 1913 by Harry Karandzieff and his best friend pete Jugaloff. They brought their confectionary skills from Greece, along with a dream of providing a friendly family environment to enjoy heir delicious creations. During the early 50's, Harry's son George took the business over and built the business into what it is today. Now George's three sons- Andy, Tommy and Mike- run the business with a little help from the fourth generation.

Our Chocolate

Did you know that Crown Candy Kitchen makes their own chocolate candy? Whether it's for a weekly candy run, to fill a Christmas stocking, for your sweetheart on Valentine's Day or for an Easter basket- Crown Candy Kitchen has been satisfying the "sweet tooth" of St. Louisans for many generations.

Our Food

Besides the soda fountain and candy, Crown Candy Kitchen serves great food for lunch and dinner. Whether you're in the mood for a thick stacked sandwich, chilli, a giant gourmet frank, a salad or a bowl of soup- Crown Candy Kitchen's staff is ready to prepare a fresh harty meal to satisfy your appetite.

Our Desserts

Don't forget to leave room for dessert! Nobody leaves Crown Candy Kitchen without grying one of our world renowned desserts. When was the last time you had a Butterscotch Malted or a Deluxe Sundae?

Before leaving, make sure to grab something for a friend, loved one or office mate! All candy, desserts and souvenirs can be packaged for takeout. You'll definitely be someone's hero!
